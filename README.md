# eleAI Studio

The definitive workstation for generative AI artists. A next-generation creative suite for high-fidelity image, video, and audio creation powered by neural engines.

## About

eleAI is a fully creator-owned, decentralized AI economy protocol built to become the definitive financial infrastructure layer for AI-native creativity.

### Mission
To build the financial infrastructure of AI creativity and empower every creator — from individuals to global brands — to fully capture the economic value generated by their imagination, labor, and digital output.

### Vision
To become the operating system and economic coordination layer for the global AI creator economy — the single place where AI-generated content is created, owned, licensed, monetized, and governed on-chain.

## Features

### Image Studio
- AI-powered image generation with multiple model support
- Aspect ratio and resolution options
- History gallery with regenerate capability

### Video Studio (Coming in Phase 2)
- AI-powered video generation
- Queue-based processing

### Audio Lab
- Text-to-speech synthesis with multiple voice options
- Voice filtering by gender, age, language, and scene
- Custom voice cloning support

### Plaza
- Community shared creations gallery
- Browse and explore AI-generated artworks

### Authentication
- Google OAuth integration
- Web3 wallet login (Ethereum, Solana)

## ELE Token

ELE is the native economic asset and utility token that powers the entire eleAI ecosystem.

### Token Supply
- Maximum Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 ELE
- Fixed supply with permanent mint disablement
- Active burn mechanisms for deflation

### Utility
- AI Consumption: Payment for image, video, audio generation
- Marketplace: Settlement for prompts, workflows, asset licensing
- Premium Membership: Higher quotas, HD exports, commercial rights
